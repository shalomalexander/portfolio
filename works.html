Automated Google Meets Attendance Tracker (GMAT 1.0)

Tech-Stack:Python, PyQT-4 Framework, Selenium

It is a Desktop Application which was created to automatically mark attendance of the student who are present in a Google Meets meeting. It utilizes web-scraping to fetch the students name. It marks PRESENT/ABSENT based on the duration of time a student is there in the meeting. When the meeting is over, it generates an excel sheet and sends the excel sheet to the inputed e-mail address.
