Electronic Health Record Management System

Tech-Stack: Python, React-JS, Django Rest Framework, PostgreSQL

Deployment-Stack: PythonAnywhere for hosting the DRF backend REST API's and Netlify for React JS Frontend

Created a web-based secure platform for Doctors, Patient and Insurance Providers to securely interact with each other. The main purpose was to reduce the hassle of physical medical records and ease of access of health reports during emergency situations using fingerprint-based access.

Automated Google Meets Attendance Tracker (GMAT 1.0)

Tech-Stack:Python, PyQT-4 Framework, Selenium

It is a Desktop Application which was created to automatically mark attendance of the student who are present in a Google Meets meeting. It utilizes web-scraping to fetch the students name. It marks PRESENT/ABSENT based on the duration of time a student is there in the meeting. When the meeting is over, it generates an excel sheet and sends the excel sheet to the inputed e-mail address.
